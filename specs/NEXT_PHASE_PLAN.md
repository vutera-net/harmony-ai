# 🚀 Harmony AI: Post-Launch Roadmap (Next Phase)

Now that the MVP and Core Ecosystem are live, the next phase focuses on transitioning from a "Functional Product" to a "Market Leader" in the spiritual AI space.

## 🎯 Strategic Goals
1. **Precision**: Move from general LLM reasoning to Expert-level Astrology (RAG).
2. **Scale**: Optimize infrastructure to handle 100k+ DAU.
3. **Monetization**: Implement real-world payment gateways and diversified tiers.
4. **Retention**: Create daily habits via energy tracking and notifications.

---

## 🛠 Technical Roadmap

### 1. AI Intelligence Upgrade (The "Deep Master" Initiative)
- **Astrology RAG**: Implement a Vector Database (e.g., Pinecone or pgvector) containing curated, high-quality Vietnamese metaphysics texts to eliminate AI hallucinations.
- **Multi-turn Memory**: Enhance the Master AI's long-term memory using a structured `aiMemory` field in the Profile to recall previous user events and life patterns.
- **Determinism Layer 2.0**: Integrate more precise astrology libraries for Bát Tự (Eight Characters) and detailed palace analysis in Tử Vi.

### 2. Infrastructure & Performance
- **Database Optimization**: 
  - Implement advanced indexing for `Chart` and `Prediction` tables.
  - Introduce Redis caching for common astrology calculations and lucky day calendars.
- **Edge Deployment**: Optimize Next.js middleware and static generation (ISR) to reduce TTFB to < 1s globally.
- **Security Hardening**: Full audit of the SSO system and encryption of sensitive birth data.

### 3. Monetization & Business Growth
- **Real Payment Integration**: Replace mock payments with VNPay, Momo, and Stripe for global access.
- **Subscription Tiers**:
  - **An Nhiên (Basic)**: Monthly Master AI chat, basic reports.
  - **Bình An (Premium)**: Weekly deep analysis, unlimited journal verification, 1 PDF report/month.
  - **Thiên Định (VIP)**: Lifetime access, bespoke PDF art reports, priority AI response.
- **PDF Automation**: Enhance the PDF generator to include dynamic art and personalized cosmic maps.

### 4. New Feature Set
- **Daily Energy Score**: A "Cosmic Weather" forecast for the user's specific energy type, updated daily.
- **Cosmic Compatibility**: Feature to analyze the synergy between two birth charts (Love/Business matching).
- **Destiny Alerts**: Push notifications for critical transit periods (e.g., "Your energy is peaking in Career today").
